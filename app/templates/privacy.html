Privacy Terms

Collection and Processing of personal Data

All the particular information that relate to a natural person is personal data. That means every information about your identity, e.g. name or e-mail address.

Your acceptance

There will be no automated collection, processing or usage of personal data without your explicit consent. Your data will only be collected if you register for the Buddy Program.

Use of your data

The RIA will only collect, save and use your data to match you to a partner in the Buddy Program. Only the information that you provided at the registration will be used. We will pass your e-mail address on to your future partner, so you will be able to communicate with each other.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files that the computer saves on your hard drive temporarily. They are part of web technology and do not harm your computer. Although they do identify the computer that you use, cookies do not include personal data, so your privacy will be protected. You can determine if and how cookies are used in the settings of your web browser.

Links to external websites

These privacy protection terms do not apply to external websites that are listed with links on the RIA homepage. Security
